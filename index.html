about most partners

A law firm built for tech, venture, and cross-border growth.

At MOST, we focus on helping founders, companies, and investors make deals happen, avoid costly mistakes, and unlock business opportunities with clarity and confidence. We work alongside our clients as long-term partners in law and business — supporting them as they build, scale, and invest across markets.

Our perspective

We combine legal expertise, multijurisdictional experience in Europe, the US, and the UK, and an investment-driven understanding of how companies raise capital, hire, scale, and make strategic decisions. This allows us to advise on cross-border matters with a perspective that strengthens decision-making, reduces friction, and supports momentum at every stage of growth.

Our experience

Since 2016, we have been working with emerging companies, founders, and investors expanding across Europe, the US, and the UK. Over the years, we have closed 200+ funding rounds, supported 300+ businesses in scaling internationally, and advised on numerous venture, commercial, and M&A transactions. We bring practical insight into what helps companies and investors succeed — and what helps them avoid the kinds of mistakes that slow growth or weaken deals.

Our approach

Whether you are forming your first company, hiring talent, protecting IP, raising capital, acquiring a business, or making an investment, we help build the legal foundations that support your strategy. We integrate legal considerations into a broader business context, enabling smoother decision-making, clearer communication, and forward momentum.

Our mission

Our mission is to transform ideas and investment opportunities into legally sound businesses and transactions. We accompany clients through every stage of growth — helping them protect what matters most, minimise risk and avoid costly mistakes, make better decisions faster, and unlock opportunities across markets.

Our commitment

Your business will evolve — and so will our support. We are here to guide, structure, and enable what comes next, with advice grounded in experience across jurisdictions, markets, and stages of growth. MOST Partners exists to help you make better decisions, reduce friction, and build lasting value — wherever your next step takes you.
