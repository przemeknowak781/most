— our expertise —

We integrate legal considerations
into business strategy.

Six interconnected areas of support — grown with your business, adapted to your stage.

  1. 01

    Company setup & structuring

    Set up and structure businesses — locally and internationally — so you are ready to scale and raise capital.

    • Company incorporation
    • Founders' agreements
    • Cap table planning
    • Group structuring & reorganisation
    • Investor-ready legal frameworks

  2. 02

    Hiring & incentive programs

    Cover the legal side of hiring, equity plans, and working with contractors — so you build the right team with confidence.

    • Employment law & contracts
    • Contractor, advisor & consultant agreements
    • ESOPs & VSOPs design
    • Cross-border hiring & compliance
    • Handbooks & onboarding

  3. 03

    Fundraising, VC & M&A

    Through every stage of fundraising and lead M&A transactions — so deals are clear, fair, and future-proof.

    • SAFEs, convertibles & term sheets
    • Equity rounds (pre-seed → Series A/B+)
    • Venture debt & credit facilities
    • Investor negotiations & governance
    • Due diligence (buy- & sell-side)
    • M&A, SPAs & earn-outs

  4. 04

    Commercial contracts & operations

    From everyday agreements to commercial partnerships — contracts that support your product, platform, and operations.

    • Day-to-day corporate affairs & governance
    • Services agreements & licensing
    • Terms of use & privacy policies
    • Client, partner & supplier agreements
    • Joint ventures & collaborations
    • GDPR & data protection

  5. 05

    IP & technology

    Protect what you build — covering IP ownership, licensing, confidentiality, and open-source risk.

    • IP strategy & ownership structuring
    • Software licensing & IP assignment
    • NDAs & confidentiality
    • Trademark filings
    • Open-source risk analysis

  6. 06

    Global expansion

    Guide your international growth — foreign entities, global contracts, and cross-border compliance.

    • Legal structuring for international growth
    • Foreign entities & subsidiaries
    • Coordination with local counsel
    • Cross-border contracts & risk allocation
    • Tax & employment alignment
    • Market entry strategies
